# Zach-Sten_Computational_Immunologist

Welcome to my Github! My name is Zachary Stensland. I'm a PhD student whos thesis work explores the immunological features associated with progression from premalignat lesions to cancer.

My work emphasizes how statistical modeling and machine learning can be applied to spatial proteomics and transcriptomics data to identify clinically relevant features and gain mechanistic insights into biology. Apart from my thesis work I am currently working with other members of the UCSF community to build a spatial-omics preprocessing and analysis pipeline; enabling more researchers to make the most of their spatial datasets and grow the community of tissue immunologists.

Research interests:
- Cancer Immunology
- Autoimmunity
- Tissue organization, development, and evolution
- Predictive modeling
- Graph Machine Learning and Network Biology
